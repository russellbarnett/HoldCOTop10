Strategic Differentiators vs. Publicis Leona

Publicis Leona is the most advanced agentic content engine released by a holdco to date. BrandStudios.AI plays a different role: a human-led creative brain that can sit above or beside any operating system, including Leona.

◆

Brand Truth vs. Performance Variables

Leona optimizes creative attributes as variables against performance. BrandStudios.AI treats creative as brand truth to be protected, learning from human approvals instead of only from clicks and conversions.

◆

Human Direction vs. Human Supervision

Leona uses specialist AI agents in the background with humans supervising output. BrandStudios.AI keeps humans in the director's seat, with AI supporting judgment and workflow instead of driving the work.

◆

Platform-Agnostic by Design

Leona is tightly coupled to the Publicis stack—CoreAI, Epsilon, production, and media. BrandStudios.AI connects into any CRM, CMS, DAM, or analytics environment, acting as an independent layer of creative intelligence across ecosystems.
